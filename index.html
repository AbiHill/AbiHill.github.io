web developer | london

Hello, my name's Abi. I'm a full stack Web Developer & Digital Designer from London.

Previously, I worked in Digital Marketing and CRM for 6 years for brands such as Cath Kidston and Buyagift. During my previous career I was happiest when working with the development team so I decided to teach myself HTML, CSS and JavaScript. To expand upon my coding skillset I carried out the Web Developement Immersive Course with General Assembly where I gained a great understanding of full stack developement. I've developed sites for clients such as Randy's Wing Bar, Empowered Living and Hazey Jane Films.

Aside from web dev, I run an online magazine called Nonchalant London which I set up as a blog in 2015. After gaining a lot of followers, NL progressed in to a business in 2016 and now has a small team of writers that drink too much coffee.
